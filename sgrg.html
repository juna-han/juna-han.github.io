Duration

Supervised by
Supervisors from HMI & UX and DBP Digital Business departments
Project type
User experience concept for Saint-Gobain Research Germany
Description
Envisioning the role of future automotive glazing with considerations of market trends, UX, and technical feasibility, brought into a cohesive concept vehicle design.
Brief
Develop a UX-focused concept vehicle design based on the winning concept of the brief: “As a user, how do you envision interacting with your vehicle glazing in 2035?”
